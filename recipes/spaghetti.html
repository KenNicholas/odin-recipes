Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish that is simple yet delicious. It consists of long, thin noodles served with a variety of sauces and toppings. The dish is a staple of Italian cuisine and is enjoyed worldwide for its versatility and comforting flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti noodles
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the tomato sauce.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
